FOOD AND BEVERAGE SERVICE IN MALAYSIA 2022

This compares key 2022 and 2015 statistics for Malaysia's food and beverage services industry, based on DOSM data. The purpose is to show industry trends and changes in Malaysia's food and beverage services between 2015 and 2022 for informed decision-making.

The number of establishments in 2022 declined by 2.9% with 136453 establishments compared to 167490 establishments in 2015. This number of decrease is due to the outbreak of Covid-19 that hit the country starting in 2020. However, the other factor has positive growth in the food and beverage service sector. The radar chart besides show the calculated average growth rate of each sector in the food and beverage service industry except the number of establishments. The activity included food services, event catering services and beverage services. Food services demonstrated the strongest overall performance.
